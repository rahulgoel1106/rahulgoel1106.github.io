I am a Ph.D. student (Junior Researcher) in the Computational Social Science Group, at the Institute of Computer Science, University of Tartu, Estonia. My research interests include Social media data, and network analysis using data science, network science and machine learning techniques. I have experience dealing with social well-being problems such as the MeToo movement, social segregation, anomaly detection, and COVID-19 utilizing social media data and/or call data records.
